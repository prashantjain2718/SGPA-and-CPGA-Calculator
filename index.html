About this RTU CGPA Calculator

This RTU CGPA calculator uses RTU's official grade-to-point mapping to calculate SGPA for a semester and compute updated CGPA by combining previous earned credits and the current semester. Enter your previous CGPA and the calculator will compute the new cumulative GPA automatically.

How SGPA & CGPA are calculated

SGPA is the weighted average of grade points for subjects in a semester: sum(grade_point × credits) ÷ sum(credits). CGPA is calculated across multiple semesters using total earned grade points divided by total credits. This tool automates both calculations using RTU credit weights.

RTU Grade mapping

The grade scale used: A++ (10), A+ (9), A (8.5), B+ (8), B (7.5), C+ (7), C (6.5), D+ (6), D (5.5), E+ (5), E (4), F (0).
